Our Services

TalentStack offers wide range of services for all our clients hiring needs.
We cater to & believe in giving end to end recruitment services to all all our clients.

Contingent Search

In this model we charge the clients based on the success. In other words the client is only liable to pay TalentStack if a candidate gets an offer and starts working for their company. This model works on a fixed percentage based fees based on the position & other factors.

Retained Search

In this model we charge the clients an upfront fee to work on the mandates. This usually works the best for small businesses & startups. We provide 100% placement guarantee in the pre discussed time frame.

Fixed Cost

In this model we charge the clients a fixed lumpsum cost instead of charging the fees based on the annual CTC. This works the best with junior level roles & bulk hiring where the volume & churn is high.

RPO

This model is known as Recruitment Process Outsourcing. This model works the best for companies who are either looking to hire a lot of employees in a limited time frame or companies outside India trying to save their cost of hiring an expensive Recruiter full time. There is a 100% dedicated Recruiter available for the client 24x7 who caters to all their open requirements.
